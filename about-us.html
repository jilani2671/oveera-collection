The Art of Draping

Weaving tradition into modernity, one thread at a time. Experience the heritage of India's finest handlooms.

Our Story

Born from a love for the six yards of grace.

It began with a simple sketch and a deep appreciation for the weavers of Banaras. We noticed that while the world moved fast, the timeless elegance of a saree remained constant.

Our journey is one of reviving forgotten motifs and blending them with contemporary palettes. We travel across India to find the finest silks, ensuring that every piece you wear tells a story of heritage and passion.

Our Craft

Weaving Magic,
Thread by Thread.

Sourced from the heart of Banaras, Kanjeevaram, and Chanderi, our weavers create magic with every shuttle movement. The rhythmic clatter of the loom is the heartbeat of our brand.

We work with over 500 artisan families, ensuring their skills are preserved and celebrated globally.
